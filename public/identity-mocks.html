Option 1 · Two-column split — easy half / work half
Frames the panel as the choice it really is: one half transfers free (OIDC), the other half is the rotation backlog (PAT). The concentration list lives inside the work column so the spatial story matches the editorial story.
Identity surface
How many service connections the program has — saved logins a team’s pipelines (automated build/deploy jobs) use to reach external systems like Azure, AWS, and package registries. Each connection uses one of two authentication methods, and only one of them transfers to GitHub without manual work. 1,847 total · across 142 teams.
