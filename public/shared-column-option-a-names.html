My pick: Variant 2 (two-column grid). With 32 entries it stays scannable in ≈16 lines, IDs align in their own column for quick eye-tracking, and names truncate gracefully on narrow widths.

Variant 1 is fine when lists are small (≤ 5) but becomes a wall of comma-separated text fast. Variant 3 looks busy and feels like a tag cloud at this size.
