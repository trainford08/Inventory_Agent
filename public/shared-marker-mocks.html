Recommendation
Ship Option B (count pill + popover). It's the only one that: scales to 20+ references without crowding, keeps the always-visible signal that "this is shared", and matches the pattern devs already know from GitHub, Linear, Notion. Pair it with Option E's subtle row tint as a passive visual cue if you want the row to also be scannable from a distance.
